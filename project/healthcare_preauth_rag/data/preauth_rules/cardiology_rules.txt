# Cardiology Prior Authorization Rules

## Echocardiography (CPT 93306, 93307, 93308)
**Preauth Required:** Yes
**Documentation Needed:**
- Clinical indication (chest pain, murmur, dyspnea, etc.)
- Recent EKG results
- Relevant medical history (HTN, DM, CAD)
**Review Time:** 2-3 business days
**Valid For:** 90 days from approval

## Cardiac Catheterization (CPT 93454, 93455, 93456)
**Preauth Required:** Yes
**Documentation Needed:**
- Abnormal stress test or imaging results
- Failed medical management documentation
- Risk assessment scores
- Recent lipid panel and renal function
**Review Time:** 3-5 business days
**Valid For:** 60 days from approval

## EKG/Electrocardiogram (CPT 93000, 93005, 93010)
**Preauth Required:** No
**Notes:** Routine diagnostic EKG does not require prior authorization

## Stress Test - Nuclear (CPT 78451, 78452)
**Preauth Required:** Yes
**Documentation Needed:**
- Symptoms (chest pain, dyspnea on exertion)
- Cardiovascular risk factors
- Inability to exercise (for pharmacologic stress)
**Review Time:** 2-3 business days
**Valid For:** 90 days from approval

## Holter Monitor (CPT 93224, 93225, 93226)
**Preauth Required:** Yes (for >48 hours)
**Documentation Needed:**
- Symptom diary requirements
- Previous EKG findings
- Clinical indication (palpitations, syncope, arrhythmia evaluation)
**Review Time:** 1-2 business days
**Valid For:** 30 days from approval
