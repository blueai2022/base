# Radiology/Imaging Prior Authorization Rules

## MRI - Lumbar Spine (CPT 72148, 72149, 72158)
**Preauth Required:** Yes
**Documentation Needed:**
- Duration of symptoms (must be >6 weeks for non-trauma)
- Conservative treatment attempted (PT, medications)
- Red flag symptoms if present (weakness, bowel/bladder changes)
- Recent plain film X-ray results
**Review Time:** 2-3 business days
**Valid For:** 90 days from approval

## MRI - Brain (CPT 70551, 70552, 70553)
**Preauth Required:** Yes
**Documentation Needed:**
- Clinical indication (headache characteristics, neurologic deficits)
- Recent neurological exam findings
- Prior imaging if available
**Review Time:** 2-3 business days
**Valid For:** 90 days from approval

## CT Scan - Chest (CPT 71250, 71260, 71270)
**Preauth Required:** Yes
**Documentation Needed:**
- Clinical indication (PE rule-out, lung nodule follow-up, etc.)
- Recent chest X-ray results
- Risk factors or symptoms
**Review Time:** 1-2 business days
**Valid For:** 60 days from approval

## X-Ray - Standard (CPT 71046, 73000, 73510)
**Preauth Required:** No
**Notes:** Routine diagnostic X-rays do not require prior authorization

## PET Scan (CPT 78811, 78812, 78813, 78814, 78815, 78816)
**Preauth Required:** Yes
**Documentation Needed:**
- Cancer diagnosis with staging information
- Prior imaging results (CT/MRI)
- Treatment plan implications
- Oncologist consultation notes
**Review Time:** 3-5 business days
**Valid For:** 30 days from approval

## Ultrasound - Abdominal (CPT 76700, 76705)
**Preauth Required:** No (diagnostic)
**Preauth Required:** Yes (screening)
**Documentation Needed for Screening:**
- Risk factors for condition being screened
- Family history if applicable
**Review Time:** 1-2 business days
