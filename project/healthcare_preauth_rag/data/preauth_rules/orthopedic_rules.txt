# Orthopedic Prior Authorization Rules

## Knee Replacement - Total (CPT 27447)
**Preauth Required:** Yes
**Documentation Needed:**
- X-rays showing severe arthritis (Kellgren-Lawrence Grade 3-4)
- Conservative treatment documentation (minimum 3 months):
  - Physical therapy records
  - Trial of NSAIDs or other pain medications
  - Intra-articular injections if applicable
- Functional limitation assessment
- BMI documentation (may require weight optimization if >40)
**Review Time:** 5-7 business days
**Valid For:** 180 days from approval

## Hip Replacement - Total (CPT 27130)
**Preauth Required:** Yes
**Documentation Needed:**
- X-rays showing severe arthritis or AVN
- Conservative treatment failure documentation (3-6 months)
- Pain scale and functional limitation assessment
- Medical clearance for surgery
**Review Time:** 5-7 business days
**Valid For:** 180 days from approval

## Arthroscopy - Knee (CPT 29881, 29880)
**Preauth Required:** Yes
**Documentation Needed:**
- MRI results showing meniscal tear or other pathology
- Failed conservative management (PT, injections)
- Mechanical symptoms (locking, catching)
**Review Time:** 3-5 business days
**Valid For:** 90 days from approval

## Spinal Fusion - Lumbar (CPT 22558, 22612, 22630)
**Preauth Required:** Yes
**Documentation Needed:**
- Advanced imaging (MRI showing specific pathology)
- Failed conservative treatment (minimum 6 months):
  - Physical therapy
  - Medications
  - Epidural injections
- Functional impairment documentation
- Neurosurgeon or spine surgeon evaluation
**Review Time:** 7-10 business days
**Valid For:** 180 days from approval

## Physical Therapy (CPT 97110, 97112, 97116)
**Preauth Required:** Yes (after initial evaluation)
**Documentation Needed:**
- Diagnosis and treatment plan
- Functional goals
- Frequency and duration request
**Review Time:** 1-2 business days
**Valid For:** Number of visits approved (typically 6-12 visits)
**Notes:** Initial PT evaluation (CPT 97161-97163) does not require preauth
